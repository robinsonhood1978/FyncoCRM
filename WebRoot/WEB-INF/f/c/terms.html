The following conditions pertain to the Bank Payments from the Taiji account holder to Taiji, (Little Crypto Limited):

  1. Bank Payment Bank Account:
    Bank payments from Taiji Account holders are made directly to our bank account, named "Little Crypto Limited”.
  2. Bank Payment Maximum Amount and Frequency:
    Taiji receives payments of between $10 - $2000NZ a week into the Taiji bank account.

    Multiple payments can be made in a week and will be combined into a single transaction for delivery as long as it is under the maximum four weekly amount of $2000.

    There is no minimum frequency, if no payment is received into the Taiji bank account, then there is no service provided. Hence, payment can be made at anytime into the Taiji bank account, as long as the payment is above $20NZ and below $2000 in that given week (7 days), and below $2000 in the four week time frame.

    Bank payments must include the Taiji Account holder’s unique Taiji number as the bank reference. Bank payments not showing the Taiji Account holder’s unique Taiji number in the bank reference will be refunded back to the Taiji Account holder’s bank account.
  4. Incorrect Bank Payment Reference Number:
    Bank payments from the Taiji Account holder that have an incorrect unique Taiji number in the bank reference will be refunded back to the Taiji Account holder’s bank account.
  5. Bank payments over maximum $ value:
    Bank payments received from the Taiji Account holder that are over $2000 NZD in that given week (7 days), will be processed up to the $2000 NZD maximum and anything over $2000 NZD refunded back to the Taiji Account holder’s bank account.
  6. Bank payments under minimum $ value:
    Bank payments received from the Taiji Account holder that are under $20NZ in that given week (7 days), will be refunded back to the Taiji Account holder’s bank account.
  7. Bank payments that have missed the Buying Timeline:
    Bank payments that miss or are too late for the Monday Buying timeline are included in the following weekly Monday buy.
